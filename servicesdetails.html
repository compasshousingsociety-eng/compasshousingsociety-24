Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our villa sales service

How long does it take to sell a villa?

On average, luxury villas sell within 3-6 months. However, this varies based on location, pricing, and market conditions. Our marketing strategy aims to find qualified buyers within 60 days.

What is included in the staging consultation?

Our staging consultation includes a walkthrough with a professional stager who provides recommendations on furniture arrangement, decluttering, and minor improvements to maximize appeal.

Do you handle international buyers?

Yes, we actively market to international buyers through our global network and partnerships with luxury real estate agencies worldwide. We also assist with international transaction requirements.

What is the commission structure?

Our standard commission is 5% for sellers and 2.5% for buyers. This includes full-service marketing, showings, negotiations, and closing support. Custom packages are available.
